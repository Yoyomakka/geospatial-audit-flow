# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while making room for correction.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Respect differing levels of GIS, Python, and open-source experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, sustained disruption, and publishing private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community. Reports can be sent to the project maintainers through the repository contact channels.
